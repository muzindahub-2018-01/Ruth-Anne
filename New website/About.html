About us

Kuvunja Ukimya is a non-governmental organisation, founded in Harare-Zimbabwe, that aims to promote education amongst women, allow women the equal opportunities to better themselves. It also aims to inspire young girls to be bold, smart and confident. To help girls fight back against gender stereotypes and myths that bring them down. As a feminist movement, we not only focus on the female as a being but all things related to femininity. We focus on gender related issues, race related issues and sexual orientation related issues, only to mention a few.We also want to empower girls digitally. “Digital literacy is becoming is important as traditional literacy. When girls and woman have access to technology and digital training they thrive. But when they are held back it can affect every aspect of their lives.” -Plan Interntional

We are concerned with:

  • Gender stereotypes
  • African myths that disempower African women
  • Rape
  • Early marriages
  • Education for the girl child

Mission Statement

The Kuvunja Ukimya aims to strengthen the already existing fight for equality amongst the human race. to run the race, with already like minded institutes, globally, towards the finish line, for the grand prize. Equality. Our mission is to have a platform were like-minded people can interact and share ideas on how to win the race. A plateform for where women and men reach mutual understanding and do not succumb to gender roles. A platform for youth engagement to give hope and understanding to what it really means to be a ferminist in the 21st century, a place where the 'F' word is considered negative and dirty. Our mission, is to win the race, and restore faith in humanity. The youth of today predict life that the youth of tomorrow will live, and because of this we will engage in a lot of youth led projects. Telling them is not enough, watching them live it, will get the messagge across. Because"the children are the future".
